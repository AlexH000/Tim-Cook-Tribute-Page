Tim Cook

Early Years

Tim Cook was born November 1, 1960 in Mobile Alabama. He pursued a industrial enginnering degree from Auburn Univiersity. After graduating he continued his education, recieving his masters degree from Duke Univiersity.

Pre-Apple:

  1. IBM
  2. Intelligent Electronics
  3. Compaq

Apple:

Despite holding the COO position at Compaq, when Cook recieved an invitation from Jobs he quickly decided to join the team as VP.

  1. 2007: Lead Operations
  2. 2009: Chief Executive
  3. 2011: CEO
